Dog Care Tips

  • Provide fresh water and nutritious food daily.
  • Take your dog for regular walks and exercise.
  • Regular vet check-ups help prevent diseases.
  • Grooming and brushing keep their coat healthy.
  • Train your dog with patience and positive reinforcement.
