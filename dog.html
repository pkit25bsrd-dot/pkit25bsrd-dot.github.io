Introduction

Dogs are loyal and friendly animals, known as man's best friend. They come in different breeds, sizes, and personalities. Dogs are often kept as pets, working animals, or service animals.

Popular Dog Breeds

Some popular dog breeds include Labrador Retriever, German Shepherd, Beagle, Golden Retriever, and Pug. Each breed has unique characteristics and traits.
