Our Story

Koseli Ghar started from scratch by four passionate sisters who wanted to share the authentic flavors of Nepal. From humble beginnings, making pickles in their own kitchen, to now processing varieties of pickles and flavored chips, they have built a name known for taste, love, and quality. Every jar and every chip is a story of tradition and effort.
